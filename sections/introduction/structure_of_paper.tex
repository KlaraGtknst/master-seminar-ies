To address these challenges, 
this review provides an comprehensive examination of various \ac{cl} approaches, 
focusing on their foundational principles and the methods employed for pair selection,

% structure
The structure of this paper is organized as follows:
\Section{sec:sampling_techniques} reviews various sampling techniques for identifying sample pairs in \ac{cl}.
\Section{sec:critic} presents a critical analysis of these techniques. 
Finally, \Section{sec:own_ideas} proposes a set of hypothetical approaches to \ac{cl}, 
along with a discussion of their potential limitations.
